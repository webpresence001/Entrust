About Entrust

Why Work Here?

Entrust Manufacturing Technologies, Inc. is headquartered in Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin, and provides the industry with high value machine tools, automation, and engineering services.

Entrust has developed substantial capabilities related to specialized engineering and manufacturing of complex, precision machinery and automated systems.

Currently, Entrust employees spend the majority of efforts on the UNISIG Deep Hole Drilling Machines division. Visit www.unisig.com to view products and learn more.
