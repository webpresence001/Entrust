History of Entrust

Follow Entrust Manufacturing Technologies from its roots as a family-owned tool and die maker to the globally recognized machine tool it is today.

  • 1982
  • 1989

  • 1995

  • 2011
  • 2012

  • 1973

    Entrust is founded as Entrust Tool and Design Co., Inc. by John Korosec in Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin. Entrust produced specialized machines and provided contract manufacturing services.

  • 1982

    First facility expansion for increased assembly and machine shop capacity

  • 1989

    Second facility expansion for large system assembly

  • 1994

    Current CEO Anthony Fettig joins Entrust's engineering department

  • 1995

    Entrust acquires UNISIG and begins to invest engineering resources to improve technologies and redefine performance expectations from deep hole drilling machines

  • 1997

    Current President Tanya Palmer joins Entrust as Controller

  • 2003

    Current management team in place. Growth strategy is formed to include substantial international business as well as increaseing North American markets.

  • 2009

    Broader growth, business development and stability strategies in place. Commitment by shareholders for further significant investment.

  • 2010

    Third facility expansion with 50 ton cranes and high bay assembly. Continued growth through sales in Asia and Europe.

  • 2011

    Name change to Entrust Manufacturing Technologies, Inc.; German subsidiary UNISIG GmbH founded to service European markets.

  • 2012

    Additional investments made in manufacturing technologies to increase capability and capacities in response to business demands.
