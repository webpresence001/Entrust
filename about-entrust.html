About Entrust

Entrust Manufacturing Technologies creates solutions for the manufacturing and machine tool industries, from engineering to the end product. Entrust's experience spans several industries, including automotive, aerospace, and oil & gas.

Entrust's extensive capabilities in employee talent, machining and manufacturing, and supplier network allow Entrust to create and produce superior solutions for machine tools and custom engineering and manufacturing products.

Entrust's team ranges from engineers to machinists to technicians, experienced in complex machine tool building and developing creative, effective solutions. Employees follow projects through from application analysis to installation and training, to provide excellent manufacturing solutions and a knowledgeable customer.

Machine components are designed in-house, and either manufactured in Entrust's machine shop, or procured from a carefully chosen, local supplier network. Machines are assembled in house in Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin, laser aligned, and tested before shipping and installation.

Industry Leader

Entrust Manufacturing Technologies has produced equipment that is currently in use, producing highly technical parts for globally recognized companies. Entrust works closely with both customers and suppliers to advance technology, improve knowledge base, and create solutions for deep hole drilling, metal cutting, and automation.
