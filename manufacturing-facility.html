Entrust Facility

Our state of the art manufacturing facility and reliable network of suppliers allows Entrust products to be engineered and manufactured in the USA.

Facility

  • Modern 62,000 sq ft [6,000 sq m] climate controlled facility
  • Lifting capacity up to 50 tons, with 41 ft [12.5 m] vertical clearance under crane
  • Conveniently located for domestic and international visitors
    • 30 minutes from Milwaukee General Mitchell International Airport (MKE)
    • 2 hours from Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD)

 

Manufacturing Equipment

  • CNC milling, up to 32 ton components
  • CNC machining, 5-axis
  • CNC turning
  • 3D laser inspection of large components, 40 meter range
  • Vibration analysis and assembly testing

 

Technologies

  • 3D CAD (Solidworks)
  • 3D CAM (MasterCAM)
  • 3D Inspection (SpatialAnalyzer)
  • Electrical system design (Autocad Electrical)
  • Advanced FEA and mechanical simulation software (Dassault Systems)
  • Gear tooth geometry and gear system simulation (Kissoft AG)

 

Business Systems

  • ERP/MRP (Exact Jobboss)
  • XRM (Microsofty Dynamics)
  • PDM (Dassault Systems)
